Telegram Channel Copier

Setup Instructions

  1. Add this bot as an administrator to both source and destination channels
  2. Enter the source channel (e.g., @source_channel or -1001234567890)
  3. Enter the destination channel (e.g., @destination_channel)
  4. Choose what to copy (media only or both media and text)
  5. (Optional) Set message range to copy specific messages
  6. Click "Start Copying" to begin
